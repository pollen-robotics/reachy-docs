**Construction:** 3D printed MJF Painted- 3 axial needle roller bearings  
**Power consumption:** 51W  
**Dimensions:** 662x88x73mm  

## Weight repartition 
Overall Arm: 1670g  
Shoulder: 240g  
Upper arm: 610g  
Forearm: 590g  
Gripper: 230g  

**Maximum payload: 500g**   
*(this may vary depending on the holding and duration configuration)*
